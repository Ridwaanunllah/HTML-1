Banana

A banana is a berry from a botanical point of view. Berries are fruits that come from one flower and usually have several seeds. Bananas are rich in potassium and other important minerals and vitamins that help your body perform critical functions. Their potential benefits include improving your digestion and heart health, thanks to their fiber and antioxidant content. Plus, they may support weight loss because they're relatively low in calories, nutrient-dense, and filling
